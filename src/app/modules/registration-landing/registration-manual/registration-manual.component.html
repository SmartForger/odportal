How do I view my surveys?

To view surveys assigned to you, go to your dashboard and find the My Surveys widget. If it is not present, you may click the three dots next to your dashboard name, choose Edit Dashboard, then click the blue + icon to open the widget modal. You may find and click the My Surveys widget to launch freefloating, or click the options button when hovering to “Add to dashboard”.
